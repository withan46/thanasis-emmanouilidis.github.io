Generative · Deep RL

Transformer GAN + CLIP-Guided RL Fine-Tuning

A conditional GAN with a from-scratch transformer text encoder for caption-to-image generation on Oxford Flowers, extended with a deterministic policy-gradient stage that uses CLIP as the reward signal. CLIP alignment improved from 0.252 to 0.520 (+106%) while keeping image realism — with analysis of the alignment–diversity tradeoff as an RLHF parallel.

Multi-Agent · RAG

Multi-Agent Information Search & Synthesis

A LangGraph pipeline of parallel web-search and RAG agents feeding a synthesis workflow, with a voting sub-graph of strict / lenient / devil's-advocate reviewers to resolve disagreement, plus a synthesizer and fact-checker behind a Streamlit UI. The report covers the agent roles, state management, and how the design maps to classical multi-agent theory (BDI, FIPA, Contract Net).

A conditional GAN with a transformer text encoder for caption-to-image generation on Oxford Flowers, extended with policy-gradient RL fine-tuning using CLIP as the reward signal.
